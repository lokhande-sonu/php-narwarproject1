Best resorts in Shivpuri - Narwar

A Luxurious Escape in the Heart of India

Narwar Lakefront Resort is a paradise for travellers who seek history, culture, and nature. The resort will offer a stunning view of a very beautiful lake and the serene Sindh River. You can enjoy spacious and elegant rooms, exquisite dining, refreshing drinks, blissful spa treatments, and invigorating swimming. You can also explore the nearby attractions, such as the famous Narwar Fort, and jungle safari in Madhav National Park or Kuno National Park.

Activities and Adventure
Discover

Camping

Enjoy the serenity of nature as you pitch a tent under the stars, start a cozy campfire, and savor a delicious barbecue. Camping is a great way to relax and reconnect with yourself and your loved ones, away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Learn More
Enjoy

Boating

Experience the thrill and tranquility of sailing on Narwar Lakefront Resort, with a boat cruise. You can enjoy the scenic views of the lush forests, the majestic castle, and the sparkling water as you glide on a speedboat, a motorboat, or a paddleboat.

Learn More
Modern

Jungle Safari

Witness the beauty and diversity of wildlife in their natural habitat, with a jungle safari. You can spot tigers, leopards, elephants, deer, birds, and many more animals as you ride in a jeep and can also learn about the flora and fauna from expert guides who will enrich your safari experience.

Learn More
Experiences

Trekking

Challenge yourself and enjoy the scenic views as you trek through. Trekking is a great way to stay fit, have fun, and discover new places. You can choose from different trekking routes that suit your level of difficulty and duration. You can also camp overnight at some of these places and enjoy the starry sky.

Learn More
Historical Dives

Fort Visit

Experience the rich history and culture of Central India with a visit to the Narwar Fort, a majestic castle built on the ramparts of Madi Kheda Dam. You can explore the fort’s architecture, which blends the styles of Rajputs, Mughals, and Marathas. The historical dives and fort visit at Narwar Lakefront Resort is a unique and thrilling way to discover the heritage and beauty of Narwar

The Perfect Venue for Your Events
Destination Events Facilities

Banquet/Conference Hall

Banquet/Conference Hall

Banquet hall: A spacious and elegant hall that can accommodate up to 200 guests for weddings, receptions, parties and other celebrations. A modern and well-furnished hall that can host up to 100 delegates for meetings, seminars, workshops and trainings.

Garden

Garden

A lush and beautiful garden that can be used for outdoor events like ceremonies, cocktails, picnics and games. The garden has a gazebo, a fountain, a swing and a fire pit. The garden also has a scenic view of the lake and the forest.

Decor Service

Decor Service

A professional and creative team that can provide customized decor solutions for any event. The team can arrange flowers, balloons, lights, candles, drapes and other accessories to suit the theme and budget of the event.

Catering Service

Catering Service

A talented and experienced team that can offer delicious and diverse cuisines for any event. The team can prepare Indian, Chinese, Continental and Italian dishes for buffet or plated service. The team can also cater to special dietary requirements like vegan, gluten-free and halal.

AudioVisual Facility

AudioVisual Facility

A reliable and efficient team that can handle all the audiovisual needs for any event. The team can provide microphones, speakers, mixers, amplifiers and other equipment for sound reinforcement. The team can also provide cameras, monitors, recorders and other equipment for video recording and live streaming.

Lakeview Fun & Enjoyment

Lakeview Fun & Enjoyment

A fun and adventurous team that can organize various activities for the guests on the lake. The team can offer boating, kayaking, fishing, sailing and other water sports for the guests. The team can also arrange bonfires, barbecues, musical nights and other entertainment for the guests.
